Our Story

Your most used product,
finally personal

Raya started with a simple question: towels are the one thing every home uses every single day — so why should they be an afterthought? The House of Namya set out to make them soft, durable, beautifully embroidered, and truly yours.

Every piece is hand-finished in small batches, with room for your initials, your colours, your story. No big-box production lines — just a small team that replies to your DM personally and gets every order right.
